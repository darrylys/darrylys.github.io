Boosty Plane

Boosty Plane is a little game where you control a plane through randomly-generated obstacle-filled world. Press, Aim, and Release to launch the plane avoiding obstacles. Your score will be the distance the plane has travelled horizontally.

Features

  • Difficulty modes.
  • Compete with other players to achieve highest score.
  • Unlock achievements.
  • Varied enemies with vastly different behavior. Try avoiding them all.
